Welcome to Trovu

This extension makes it easier to use trovu.net in your browser.

It defines keyboard shortcuts to open a new tab or window with the Trovu search console.

Settings

    Set Keyboard Shortcuts

    1. chrome://extensions/
    1. Go to about:addons.
    2. Click the gear icon ⚙️.
    3. Click Manage Extension Shortcuts.
    1. Open Safari Preferences by clicking on Safari in the menu bar and selecting Preferences.
    2. Go to the Extensions tab to see your installed extensions.
    3. Close the Preferences window and click on Safari in the menu bar again, then select Settings for This Website.
    4. Find your extension in the list and click on the field next to it to set your keyboard shortcut.
    5. Press the desired key combination to set your shortcut.
